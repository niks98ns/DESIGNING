Be a helping hand

Helping those in need around us certainly teaches us a lot.
Overcoming the tendency of selfishness and helping out a fellow
human being today will make you feel better about yourself. Simply
for a moment, you are connected to that person and this little gesture
of yours will improve the life of another person. One little step at a
time can make the world a better place.

Sharing Goodness - Each day we should try to bring goodness to the world by doing little acts of charity. A hug, a kind word, spending time, exhibiting a little kindness and being friendly is all that matters.

Changing Lives - It is something we are trying to foster as we focus on using our resources for the betterment of the society so that it positively affects our communities.

Spreading Smiles - Sometimes a simple smile can put a warm feeling in someone else's heart and make their day better. We believe giving back is important to us as a better world begins with best practices.

The Relief Convoys

Just like the realities natural disasters have changed the world we live in. These calamities adversely affect the people, their lives, and the entire nation is shaken. This is the time when affected cities are looking for support and pillars to help build all that is lost in the disaster. Nepal faced the aftermath of a massive earthquake that destroyed all and left the country and its people terrified. The tragedy hit hard to homes and, more importantly, the lives of many.


We in association with Can&Will (Social Partners) and Chandigarhmetro.com (Media Partners) started a relief camp in the Tri-City. This was a small initiative to provide help in the hardest hit areas of Nepal. While they were struggling with basic amenities, our relief convoys made it on time to provide the maximum aid.

The relief campaign was an initiative to contribute our bit to the noble cause where many joined this drive and proved that humanity still exists. Money can't buy life, but it can surely make life easier, and financial aid in time like this is one of the kindest things anyone can do.

CATS a voluntary organization works in association with their social endeavor arm, CAN & WILL Foundation that organizes blood donation camps. Zapbuild feels proud to be associated with them in this blood donation drive each year.

Nepal Rising- the sun rose & so did Nepal All our relief material reached Nepal and made it to the affected area. The smallest donations made, have helped this nation in overcoming the tough. With the assistance and efforts of people this nation has risen and fought.

Education is the future

Education is vital for a better quality of life for all children. Each year, thousands of children drop school to work and support their family hence they are robbed of their childhood. Giving someone the gift of education is the biggest deed one does in life. When you send a child to school you are not only helping him grow but a nation at the same time. Helping others helps us grow as education paves our future and when you help a child go to school you are making a positive impact where you live, work which indirectly creates a better impact on others and ourselves. Giving back is important to us. Adopt a school or sponsor a child today as knowledge is the strongest tool to conquer the biggest problems.
