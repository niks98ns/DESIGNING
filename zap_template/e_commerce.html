Magento

Magento is one of the leading ecommerce platforms that are built on open source technology. This should be able to provide marketers and online merchants with a highly customized and flexible shopping cart system that offers a seamless and fluid shopping experience to the user. It has powerful marketing tools and lets the user perform catalog-management functions and also has easily understandable search engine optimization features.

Shopify

Shopify is considered as one of the very rare and complete ecommerce solutions. This allows the marketer and business heads to set up an online store in a snap. You can easily organize your products according to size, colour, utility and many other categories. It also gives you the ability to customize your storefront, choose different payment methods, and also track and respond to orders instantly.

Wix

Wix is one of the most highly used and easiest website builders today in the market. This is a platform that offers a lot of different ways to build a website. Wix has an Artificial Design Intelligence feature built into it and the very powerful Wix Editor and Wix Code let the user create just about any kind of website.

Woocommerce

WooCommerce is ideal for you if you want to sell your products and services online. It is a platform specifically designed to make these things easier and affordable for you. It lets you sell digital and physical products. You can also manage your inventory and shipping processes and also handle your payments, and automatically sort out your taxes. WooCommerce lets you keep 100% control over all your data.
